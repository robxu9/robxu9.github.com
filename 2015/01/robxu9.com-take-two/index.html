robxu9.com, take two
in which I bet on how long I'll maintain this blog.

I never had the time to really blog about things in length after I left DigitalOcean. I guess I felt like it was time consuming and such effort could better be spent sleeping or coding or something along the lines of that.

But then school came around and I was thrown into the chaos that is the fall semester of university, and I never had a chance to step back from all the craziness that followed and put my thoughts together.

And now here we are. It’s 2015, almost 6 months since my last blog post. I’ve resolved to take more time out to reflect on what’s been happening and gather my thoughts.

I’m therefore taking bets on how quickly this will fall apart. Minimum time after I finish writing this post.
